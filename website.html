What is buttonfleX?

"Button Flex On Your Ex"

"ButtonFleX" is a business run in A.Y Jackson Secondary School that sells custom made buttons of your choice.
The company was established and founded on November 10th, 2017 by Ryan Farokhi and Isaac Blais.
Explore our website to learn more!

Our company constructs and sells various
button types, such as Pinbacks, Keychains
and Magnets, in many different shapes and
sizes. If interested in customization,
we'll make a button of your choosing, just
contact us using the information provided
on the contact us page. All of our earnings
are donated to the Humane Society to show our
support. Throughout the website we provide
useful information worth knowing, if interested
in learning more, continue to explore. Our goal
as a company is to raise enough money in order
to donate and contribute to the Humane Society,
we hope to achieve this goal, all we need is your help.
